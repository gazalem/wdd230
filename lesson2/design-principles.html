Contrast Principle

Nike Inc.

https://www.nike.com/

For the contrast principle I found the site of Nike, Inc. which take a clean and neat layout using basic color like white and black to contrast between content(headers) on images they use white font on images with black involved. The footer is also pretty good to see the differents sections of the site and the social media that has a great contrast between the black background.
